Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1lb spicy ground sausace
  • 2 cans jarred sauce of your choice
  • 1 can of spicy rotel orpetite diced tomatos
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 green pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 2 tsp italian seasonings
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 package of spaghetti noodles
  • Parmesian or shredded cheese of choice, to taste

Stove Top Instructions

  1. Heat a pan over medium-high heat
  2. Dice onion and peppers
  3. Toss onion and peppers into the pan with some olive olive
  4. After a minute or two, mix garlic into the onion and peppers
  5. Saute for another minute and then add meat to the pan
  6. Add seasonings to meat mixture and combine to taste
  7. Fill a pot with water and bring to a boil
  8. Cook meat until browned and then drain pan
  9. Once water is boiling, add pasta to water and cook until al dente
  10. Add sauce and rotel and/or tomatoes to the meat mixture
  11. Mix throroughly and reduce heat to a simmer
  12. When noodles are done, scoop 1 ladle of pasta water and add it to the sauce
  13. Drain and add noodles to the pasta sauce pan
  14. Mix well
  15. Simmer for a few minutes (if you want the sauce to simmer for longer, don't combine the noodles until the end of cooking)
  16. Serve with salad, garlic bread, and enjoy!
