Grandma's Dip

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1lb spicy ground sausage
  • 2 32oz blocks of Velveeta cheese
  • 3 cans of Rotel
  • 2 cans cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste

Crockpot Recipe Instructions

  1. Place the ground beef and spicy sausage in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Dice your onion, green pepper, and red pepper.
  3. Add your cans of rotel and cream of mushroom to the crockpot
  4. As the meat mixture starts to brown, add your peppers and onions to finish cooking.
  5. Towards the end of the browning, add your salt, pepper, red pepper flakes and garlic powder
  6. Drain the greese and then add meat mixture to the crockpot
  7. Cooking on low for 6 hours or high for 3
  8. Switch to keep warm, stir, and enjoy!
